Hi, I'm Angelbert Tacalan, a passionate digital animator and illustrator based in Naga City, Philippines. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology. I'm excited to share my journey as an aspiring animator and artist with you. Ever since I was young, animation has captivated my imagination and fueled my creativity. Growing up watching animated films and cartoons, I developed a deep appreciation for the artistry and storytelling potential of this medium. It inspired me to embark on a path of learning and exploration, Throughout my education, I have been acquiring a wide range of skills and knowledge related to digital animation and illustration. I have delved into both 2D and 3D animation, using industry-standard software to breathe life into characters and create visually stunning animations. Additionally, I have honed my illustration skills, specializing in character design and concept art.
